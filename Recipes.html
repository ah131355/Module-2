Ingredients Directions

3/4 cup packed brwon sugar Mix sugars, butter, vanilla

1 cup softened butter Stir in flour, baking soda
and salt (dough will be stiff).
Stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
1 tsp vanilla Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls

cookie sheet.
