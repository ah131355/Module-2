Gossip Girl Characters

Penn Badgley plays Dan Humphrey in the tv series
"Gossip Girl" Dan lives in Brooklyn
and throughout the show always talks about
wanting to be a part of "their world", which is how
he refers to the lives of the
other characters and his fellow classmates who
live luxuriously in Manhattan. Dan is the "outsider" of the storyline

Chace Crawford plays "Nathan Archivbald" in the show.
He comes from a very wealthy family and his father is a well-liked man in
Manhattan until he becomes incarcerated due to commiting fraud.
As Nate grows he is determined to rebuild the family name on his own.
