Gossip Girl Characters

Penn Badgley

Penn Badgley plays Dan Humphrey in the tv series
"Gossip Girl" Dan lives in Brooklyn
and throughout the show always talks about
wanting to be a part of "their world", which is how
he refers to the lives of the
other characters and his fellow classmates who
live luxuriously in Manhattan. Dan is the "outsider" of the storyline

Penn

Chace Crawford

Chace Crawford plays "Nathan Archivbald" in the show.
He comes from a very wealthy family and his father is a well-liked man in
Manhattan until he becomes incarcerated due to commiting fraud.
As Nate grows he is determined to rebuild the family name on his own.

Chace

Leighton Meester

Leighton Meester play "Blair Waldorf" who is the queen bee of the show.
She is known for her scheming and brings the drama into the plot.
Blair's struggle is finding herself meanwhile trying to live up to her
mothers name as a fashion designer in new york. Her romance with Chuck Bass
is also another important theme in the show.

Leighton

Ed Westwick

Ed Westwick plays Chuck Bass who portrays a prideful character who
thinks that he can do anything he wants and doesn't care who or what
his actions affect. He also has a tough relationship with his dad
who never talks to him because he blames Chuck for the death of
his wife, Chucks mother.

Ed

Blake Lively

Blake Lively plays the role of "Serena Van der Woodsen" who is the
it-girl of the show. Serena struggles with her past reputation as
she tries to change into a better person. She also deals with her
mother who would do anything to hide her secrets in order to
keep her reputation safe.

Blake
